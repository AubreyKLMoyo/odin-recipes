Description

Easily scrumptious, a delight!!

Ingredients

  • 4 cup vegetable oil, divided
  • 4 to 6 bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose seasoning, plus more as needed
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine oil, celery mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Heat vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt together in a medium bowl until blended. Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat.
  3. Add chicken thighs for at least 2 hours before serving.
